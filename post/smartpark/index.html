Image Recognition in Smart Parking System

10 Aug 10086, 00:00

The purpose of this project is to construct a smart parking lot which can make the parking quicker. Auto- detection of the specifically built signs is adopted to assist the parking. The open source computer vision library, OpenCV2.4, is employed in this project.

Scene ONE: How the driver obtains the location of the parking space?

Solution ONE: The QR-code which represents the unique position of the parking space is stick to the ground. The camera on the bottom of the car can decode the information after obtaining the picture. The QR-code is specially designed to resist the stain from the outer environment.


                                                                          Diagrammatic sketch

Scene TWO: How the driver parks the car correctly?

Solution TWO: The specifically built sign is stick to the bottom of the car. The real-time picture captured by the camera can tell the driver whether the car is on the right position. This technique helps the driver to park the car on the correct position.
