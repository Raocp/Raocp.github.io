Unmanned Underwater Vehicle (UUV) - FlapFish 1.0

13 Jul 2014, 00:10

###Description:###

The FlapFish 1.0, a concept UUV (Unmanned Underwater Vehicle),is designed for marine geomorphy exploration. It is approximately 0.8m long and 0.2m diameter. The major feature of the FlapFish is that it is propelled by a pair of flapping wings, which can reduce drastically the propeller-induced noise.The motor is under the control of a microcontroller which enables the FlapFish 1.0 to voyage with assigned velocity and direction.
